About Us

Homestead 1862, LLC was established with the purpose of generating, maintaining, and increasing the value and profitability of our residential investment properties. Our ability to offer complete involvement in all aspects of investment asset acquisition, ownership, management, operational and value analysis, and disposition enables us to maximize the value of our clients’ investments and aggressively generate the wealth amongst all of our business partners. This and our exclusive focus on quality of end product are what set us apart from other real estate firms who simply “fix and flip”.

CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT

Homestead 1862, LLC has a background in construction management and is qualified to manage large repair, renovation and capital improvement projects. From small scale “curb appeal” projects to extensive renovations, our consultation ensures that all contractors deliver inspection-ready, high quality work within budget constraints.
